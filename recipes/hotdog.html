Hotdog

Description

The hotdog is an all American treat that is loved by many. This processed meat food can be transformed into a ballpark nirvana by adding delictable toppings such as mustard, ketchup, or mayo.

To ensure you have the tastiest hotdog you've ever eaten follow the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog
  • Bun
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Put hotdog in water
  2. Boil for 10 minutes
  3. Put in bun
  4. Cover with desired condiments
