Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti has always been a family favorite

No matter if you are in a pinch for time or money spaghetti is always a perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Bring salted water to a boil
  2. Put noodles in and cook until al dente
  3. Brown meat, add sauce. Mix together. Enjoy
